Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get a creative prompt?

Type up to three words, feelings, or ideas into the ingredient slots and tap BLEND. Or leave them empty and let the AI surprise you!

How do I save a prompt?

When a prompt appears on the splat screen, tap the 🫙 bottle button to save it to your shelf.

How do I share a prompt?

Go to your shelf, tap the Share button on any bottled prompt to share it as an image or text.

What is Premium?

Premium is a one-time purchase of £4.99 that removes all ads forever and enables cloud shelf sync across your devices.

How do I restore my purchase?

Go to the Pricing screen and tap "Restore previous purchase".

How do I delete my account?

Go to Profile → Delete Account. This will permanently delete all your data.
