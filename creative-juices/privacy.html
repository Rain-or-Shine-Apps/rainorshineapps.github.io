1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide when creating an account, including your name and email address. We also collect creative prompts you choose to save within the app.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to provide and improve our services, sync your shelf across devices (premium users), and respond to your questions.

3. AI-Generated Content

Creative Juices uses the Anthropic Claude API to generate creative prompts. Your ingredient words are sent to Anthropic's servers to generate prompts but are not stored by us.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share it with service providers who help operate our platform (such as Supabase for cloud storage), or when required by law.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate measures to protect your personal information against unauthorised access or disclosure.

6. Your Rights

You can access, update, or delete your personal information at any time through your profile settings or by contacting us. Upon deletion, all your personal data will be permanently removed within 30 days.

7. Children's Privacy

Creative Juices is designed to be safe for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent.

8. Advertising

Free users may see ads served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers for ad personalisation. You can remove ads permanently with a one-time Premium purchase.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of changes by posting the new policy on this page.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at support@rainorshineapps.com
