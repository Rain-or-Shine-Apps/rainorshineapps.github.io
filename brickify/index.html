How it works

1

Choose a passcode

Set a 6-digit PIN you'll remember but won't enter on impulse.

2

Set a second factor

Parent PIN, authenticator app, or email code. Both factors are required to unbrick — losing one doesn't lock you out forever.

3

Save your recovery codes

Three one-time backup codes are generated. Write them down. If you ever lose your PIN and second factor, one of these codes will get you back in.

4

Select apps to block

Pick exactly which apps are disabled when bricked. Everything else stays accessible.

5

Tap BRICK

Your chosen apps are blocked at the OS level. To unblock them, you need both your PIN and your second factor.

Known limitations & accepted risks

Brickify is designed to make bypassing meaningfully hard — not impossible. Here is an honest account of what is and isn't protected against.

Android

Accepted risks

Safe mode — Android's safe mode disables third-party services. Blocks will not be enforced while in safe mode. Parent email is warned.

ADB over USB — If USB debugging is enabled, someone with a PC could remove the app or disable services.

Factory reset — Removes all restrictions. Android's Factory Reset Protection (FRP) mitigates this. We recommend enabling FRP by adding a Google account and screen lock.

New apps — Apps installed after setup are not automatically blocked. You will receive a notification and email when a new app is installed.

Hardened against

Uninstalling the app — Brickify registers as a Device Administrator, graying out the uninstall button.

Force stopping — Device Administrator registration also prevents force stop.

Revoking the Accessibility permission — A background watchdog runs every 15 minutes. If the service is disabled while bricked, the screen is locked immediately.

iOS

Accepted risks

Parent Apple ID known to child — Family Controls is tied to the parent's Apple ID. A strong, unique Apple ID password is recommended.

Restoring from iCloud backup — Restoring a pre-Brickify backup removes the app. See the uninstall section below for what this means in practice.

Factory reset — Removes all restrictions. A Screen Time passcode (set up during Brickify's setup flow) prevents a reset being initiated through Settings.

Hardened against

Deleting the app — The setup guide walks you through enabling a Screen Time passcode with app deletion blocked.
