iOS note: If Brickify has been deleted from an iPhone, the app blocks remain active — iOS enforces Family Controls independently of the app. To lift them you need to reinstall Brickify, or contact us so we can guide you through Screen Time settings.

Common questions

Can the child just delete Brickify?

Android: No — Brickify registers as a Device Administrator, which prevents the app being uninstalled or force-stopped.

iOS: Not if you followed the setup guide to enable a Screen Time passcode with app deletion blocked. If deletion does occur, the app blocks remain active.

What if a new app is installed?

On Android, Brickify posts a notification on the device and emails the parent's notification address (if set up). Open Brickify and add the new app to the block list if needed.

Does Brickify work without internet?

Yes — app blocking, PIN verification, and recovery code checks all work completely offline. Internet is only needed for email 2FA codes and the new-app email notification.

Can I change my 2FA method after setup?

Yes — go to Settings inside the app to reconfigure your second factor.

Accessibility

Brickify is designed to be usable by everyone, including users who rely on assistive technologies.

👁️

VoiceOver (iOS) & TalkBack (Android)

All interactive elements have descriptive accessibility labels. The PIN keypad announces each digit and the current count of digits entered. Errors and lockout countdowns are read aloud automatically.

🔤

Dynamic text size

The app respects your system font size setting. Text scales across all screens without breaking layouts.

🎨

High contrast & dark mode

Brickify adapts to your system dark mode. All text meets WCAG AA contrast ratios in both light and dark modes.

🔇

No audio-only cues

The app does not rely on sound to convey information. All feedback is visual and available to screen readers. Fully usable with sound off or by deaf users.

📱

Switch control & keyboard navigation

All interactive elements are reachable via Switch Control (iOS) and external keyboards. Focus order follows a logical top-to-bottom sequence.

🚨

Emergency access never blocked

The emergency call button on the bricked screen is always visible and accessible regardless of bricked state or accessibility settings.

Reporting an accessibility issue

Email rain.or.shine.apps@gmail.com with a description of the issue and the assistive technology you are using. We will respond within 5 business days.

Platform requirements

  • iOS: iOS 16 or later. App blocking uses Apple Family Controls — no background running required.
  • Android: Android 8.0 or later. App blocking uses the Android Accessibility Service — must be enabled in Settings → Accessibility → Brickify.
  • Both platforms require a physical device. App blocking does not function in simulators or emulators.
